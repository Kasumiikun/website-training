The official Global Taiko Showdown website!

The Global Taiko Showdown is an osu!taiko tournament series, organized on the free-to-play rhythm game, osu!. It is one of the biggest osu!taiko tournament series, if not the biggest, and it is fully organized by the GTS Team. The tournaments we organize are:

  • the Intermediate Global Taiko Showdown
  • the Expert Global Taiko Showdown
  • the Advanced Global Taiko Showdown
  • the Beginners Global Taiko Showdown

We hope to bring you even better tournaments in the future!
